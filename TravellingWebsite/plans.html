Goa Plans

Goa, located on the western coast of India, is one of the country's most popular tourist destinations. Known for its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, rich culture, and historical landmarks, Goa offers a unique blend of Indian and Portuguese cultures, due to its history as a Portuguese colony until 1961.Goa is a paradise for beach lovers, history enthusiasts, foodies, and adventure seekers alike. With its laid-back vibe, welcoming locals, and myriad attractions, Goa promises an unforgettable experience for every traveler.

Places to visit

Calangute Beach

Calangute is a town in the North Goa district of the Indian state of Goa. It is famous for its beach, the largest in North Goa and a popular tourist destination.

Special

Calangute Beach offers a plethora of activities for visitors, including water sports, sunbathing, and beachside dining. The beach is lined with colourful shacks serving delicious seafood and refreshing beverages. The bustling nightlife, featuring lively bars and clubs, adds to the beach's charm.

Basilica of Bom Jesus

The Basilica of Bom Jesus (Portuguese: Basílica do Bom Jesus; Konkani: Borea Jezuchi Bajilika) is a Catholic basilica located in Goa, in the Konkan region of India. The iconic church is a pilgrimage centre and recognised by UNESCO as a World Heritage Site.

Special

This Jesuit church is India's first minor basilica, and is considered to be one of the best examples of baroque architecture and Portuguese colonial architecture in India. It is one of the Seven Wonders of Portuguese Origin in the World.

Fort Aguada

Fort Aguada is a well-preserved seventeenth-century Portuguese fort, along with a lighthouse, standing in Goa, India, on Sinquerim Beach, overlooking the Arabian Sea. It is an ASI protected Monument of National Importance in Goa.

Dudhsagar Falls

Dudhsagar Falls (lit. 'Sea of Milk') is a four-tiered waterfall on the Mandovi River in the Indian state of Goa. It is 60 km from Panaji by road and is located on the Belgaum–Vasco Da Gama rail route about 46 km east of Madgaon and 80 km south of Belgaum.

Club Cubana

Get ready for a night of funky vibes and pulsating beats at Club Cubana. Undoubtedly Asia’s number one Night Club, located atop Arpora hill, this “Nightclub in the Sky” Club Cubana, is “the” place to party throughout the week.

Manali Plans

Manali, nestled in the Kullu Valley in Himachal Pradesh, is a popular hill station known for its breathtaking landscapes, adventure activities, and serene environment. Situated at an altitude of 2,050 meters (6,726 feet) in the Himalayas, Manali offers a perfect getaway for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life.

Manali is a paradise for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and those looking for a peaceful retreat. With its stunning landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and plethora of activities, Manali promises an unforgettable experience for every traveler. Whether you're trekking through scenic trails, indulging in local cuisine, or simply soaking in the serene mountain views, Manali has something to offer for everyone.
